Jalepeno Popper Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2oz softened cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp sour cream
  • 10 pickled jalepeno pepper slices - chopped
  • 2 ciabatta sandwich rolls
  • 4 tsp butter
  • 8 tortilla chips, crushed
  • 1/2 cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1 Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and pickled jalapeno in a small bowl. Set aside.
  2. Step 2 Slice each roll in half, slice the tops off. Spread 1 teaspoon butter on both sides. Place 1/2 of the cream cheese mixture, 1/2 of the crushed chips, and 1/2 of the shredded cheese on the bottom bun.
  3. Step 3 Grill until lightly browned and flip over, about 3 to 5 minutes; continue grilling until cheese is melted and the second side is golden brown.
